BIO

I'm 41 years old and recently divorced. I have two boys. Jonas is 7, and Miles is 4. As part of the rebuilding of my life, I am transitioning into a career as a programmer. I enjoy the puzzle solving aspect of coding, and I enjoy working collaboratively with others.

I grew up in Wausau, a small town in north central Wisconsin. I got a business degree from the University of Wisconsin - Madison. If you root for the Bears, I apologize in advance, but we might not get along (just kidding (mostly)).

After college, I headed out west to the mountains. I brewed beer professionally for a while. I was a massage therapist for a while. I was a real estate investor for a while. Now I will write code.

Interests include snow skiing, water skiing, aikido, long naked walks in the tall grass, cooking, reading, mixing cocktails, watching movies, exploring the esoteric and philosophic mysteries of the universe, squanching.
